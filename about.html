About VanishGames

Hey there, welcome to VanishGames! The best part? No ads, ever.

Honestly, I got tired of all those slow, clunky game sites that our school Chromebooks just couldn’t handle. So, I decided to make something simple, fast, and fun – without all the annoying ads getting in the way.

Hope you enjoy the games here at VanishGames! It’s a small collection for now, but it’s all about the fun.

Wanna support my work? Head over to this project’s GitHub Repo and leave a star – that’s all you gotta do!
